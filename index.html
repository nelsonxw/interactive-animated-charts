Possible Causes of Your Health Risks

After a preliminary review of the data from the sources, a pattern emerged to indicate some likely causes of major health risks, e.g. Obesity, Smoking and Overall Health Care Coverage. Among many factors we reviewed, poverty ratio, family median income and level of education displayed higher coorelations to the identified health risks. For example:

  • the correlation between health care coverage and education less than high school amounts to 69%
  • the correlation between median income and smoking risk amounts to 66%
  • the correlation between median income and obesity amounts to 66%
